Is It Time to Call Pest Control? Common Signs

Recognizing the early warning signs of a pest infestation can save you time, money, and potential health risks. Here are some common indicators that you may need to call pest control. If you spot any of these problems, be sure to contact pest control.

  • Visible Pests Spotting live or dead insects, rodents, or other pests in your home or business is a clear sign of an infestation.
  • Droppings Finding droppings near food storage areas, in cabinets, or along baseboards indicates the presence of pests.
  • Damage Noticing chewed wires, damaged wood, or torn fabric can be a sign of rodent or insect activity.
  • Nests Discovering nests in attics, walls, or other secluded areas confirms a pest infestation.
  • Unusual Odors Foul or musty odors can indicate the presence of pests, especially rodents or cockroaches.
  • Scratching Noises Hearing scratching sounds in walls or ceilings can be a sign of rodents or other pests moving around.
